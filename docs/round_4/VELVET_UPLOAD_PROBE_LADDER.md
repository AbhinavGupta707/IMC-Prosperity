## Decision Rules After Uploads

- If probe 1 wins and probe 2 does not: VELVET recycle/re-short is real enough
  to keep testing.
- If probes 1 and 2 both win similarly: the alpha is mostly covering the
  early short and terminal mark. Prefer conservative/delayed controls.
- If probes 3-5 score close to probe 1: reduce terminal long exposure in the
  final candidate. The official path does not need full `+200` VELVET.
- If probe 6 wins with smaller drawdown: favor delayed-gate VELVET for final
  composition even if probe 1 has higher 100k upside.
- If probe 8 wins but the negative control also wins: do not use rolling
  recycling as final logic yet.
